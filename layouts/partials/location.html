Location

Florianópolis is the capital of Brazil’s Santa Catarina state. It's often called “the best place to live in Brazil”. Florianopolis is a thriving destination for its perfect beaches, excellent surfing, amazing seafood, and juxtaposition of a modern megacity with 16th-century colonial fortresses and relaxed markets and parks. For more information please visit Florianopolis .
